   The impact of no-new-kindergarten students at LES is much more severe than simply the

elimination of one class because parents with more than one child at LES may end up with

children having to go to two schools, meaning two different drop-offs and pick-ups each day,

with one of the schools being farther away in an adjacent community. To avoid this, parents

will choose a single school that can accommodate both children.
